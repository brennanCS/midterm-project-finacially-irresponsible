Information about Mercury

Size of Mercury

Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system.

To put it into perspective Mercury is a little more than one third the size of Earth.

Diameter of 3032 miles.

Temperature on Mercury

Mercury has extreme temperature changes between day and night.

During daytime temperature reaches up to 800 degrees Fahrenheit.

During Nightime temperatures drops to -290 degrees fahrenheit.

Does mercury have any moons?

No

How long does Mercury take to complete a orbit?

To complete a full orbit around the sun it takes 88 days for Mercury.
